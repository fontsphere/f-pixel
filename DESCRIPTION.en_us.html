F Pixel Extended Extra Light is the ultra-light, extended-width style from the F Pixel family, a minimalist display system built on pure geometry and inspired by pixel art.

Designed for maximum clarity and precision, this style works perfectly in titles, posters, interfaces, and experimental layouts. Its delicate weight and extended proportions lend a modern, futuristic edge to your designs.

The full F Pixel family features 15 fonts across three widths (Normal, Wide, Extended) and five weights (ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold), offering versatile options for any project.
